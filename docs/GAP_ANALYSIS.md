# GAP_ANALYSIS ‚Äî HalalBizs Marketplace

> Cross-reference of the current codebase (`ECOMMERCE_AUDIT.md`) against world-class capabilities (`FEATURE_UNIVERSE.md`). Every gap is scored **impact** (1‚Äì5) √ó **effort** (S/M/L/XL) and names the concrete files/migrations/services it touches on this stack (Laravel 13 ¬∑ Livewire 4 ¬∑ SQLite/MySQL ¬∑ Scout). Sequenced in `ROADMAP.md`.
>
> **Scope note:** the owner re-scoped the platform from Malaysia-only to **worldwide**, so tax, e-invoicing, payments and currency are treated as *pluggable, multi-jurisdiction* concerns with Malaysia as the first concrete implementation. Existing MY rails (COD + iPay88) and MYR settlement remain the launch default.

## Executive verdict

HalalBizs is a **genuinely mature** B2C multi-vendor marketplace ‚Äî M1‚ÄìM8 are real, not aspirational. The transactional core is high-craft and money-correct: `CheckoutService` does integer-sen math end-to-end, locks variants + vouchers under `lockForUpdate` in one transaction, snapshots order items, resolves commission, and stacks vouchers with exact largest-remainder proration. The sub-order state machine, ledger/payouts, returns paperwork, reviews, OAuth/2FA/OTP, admin governance and the "Souk" redesign are all implemented and tested (~838 cases).

The gaps cluster in three honest buckets: **(Tier 1)** it cannot legally/operationally open a real store yet ‚Äî no tax, no e-invoicing, stubbed shipping, incomplete refunds; **(Tier 2)** it lacks the competitive depth that lifts conversion and retention; **(Tier 3)** it has none of the AI-native / SEA-social frontier that would make it remarkable. Critically, three "gaps" reported by automated analysis were **false** on inspection and are excluded below: the delivery auto-complete window *is* wired, `commission_rate` is a rate (not a money-rule violation), and the XML sitemap *exists*.

## What is explicitly NOT a gap (deliberate or already done)

- **Guest checkout** ‚Äî locked decision (account required, Shopee model). Not a defect.
- **Delivery auto-complete window** ‚Äî already wired (`SubOrderStatusService:82-85` + hourly job + buyer `confirmReceived()`).
- **`commission_rate` as decimal** ‚Äî a rate, converted to exact integer basis points; money stays integer sen.
- **XML sitemap** ‚Äî exists (`GenerateSitemap` command, scheduled daily).
- **Product bundles/grouped/booking types** ‚Äî intentionally out (everything is a variant; Shopee-model marketplaces don't expose these).
